# Enterprise Model Inventory — Typical Large Commercial Bank

Representative enterprise-wide model inventory in line with SR 11-7, OSFI E-23 and the EBA model definition. Rows carry a unique model ID and are grouped by Area (risk areas 1–11, non-risk areas 12–21). AIRB portfolios carry complete PD / LGD / EAD-CCF parameter suites.

**Risk tiering (materiality-based):** **Tier 1** = high materiality — direct impact on regulatory capital, published financial statements, liquidity adequacy or critical regulatory compliance; most intensive validation (typically annual full review). **Tier 2** = medium materiality — significant business decisions, customer outcomes/conduct impact, or direct feeder to Tier 1 models; full validation every 2–3 years with annual monitoring. **Tier 3** = low materiality — internal planning and operational efficiency uses with human-in-the-loop; lightest-touch oversight, validation every 3–5 years or on material change.
